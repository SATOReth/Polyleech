Frequently Asked Questions

What is PolyLeech?

PolyLeech is a Telegram bot that automatically copies trades from successful Polymarket traders to your wallet. Hold $LEECH tokens to unlock features.

How does copy trading work?

When a trader you're copying makes a trade on Polymarket, our bot instantly executes the same trade in your wallet. You get notified via Telegram.

Is my money safe?

You control your own wallet. We never have access to your private keys. The bot only executes trades on your behalf using USDC you deposit.

What are the fees?

We charge a small fee (0.25% - 1.5%) on each copied trade, depending on your tier. Higher $LEECH holdings = lower fees.

Why do I need $LEECH tokens?

$LEECH is the access token for the platform. Holding more tokens unlocks more features, more copy slots, and lower fees.

Which blockchain is used?

$LEECH token is on Solana. Trading happens on Polygon (where Polymarket operates). You need USDC on Polygon to trade.
